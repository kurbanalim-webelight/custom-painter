shader ‚Äî Gradients and pattern fills

The Paint.shader property replaces solid colour with a gradient or pattern. You create shaders from LinearGradient, RadialGradient, or SweepGradient by calling createShader(rect) and passing the result to paint.shader. Linear gradients transition along a line; radial from centre outward; sweep around a circle ‚Äî ideal for progress rings, glows, and rich fills.
