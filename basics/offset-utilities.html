Offset.distance ‚Äî Distance between points

The distance getter gives you the Euclidean distance from the origin (0, 0) to this offset ‚Äî i.e. the length of the vector. To get the distance between two points, use (offset1 - offset2).distance. Very useful for hit-testing, spacing, and ‚Äúhow far apart‚Äù logic.

Offset.direction ‚Äî Angle between points

The direction getter gives you the angle in radians from the positive X-axis to this offset (from the origin). So it tells you the direction of the vector. For the angle between two points, use (offset2 - offset1).direction. Handy for rotation, arrows, and anything that needs an angle. Already explained on the Offset page.
